The one and Only----Jordy

Hello! Welcome to my little corner of the internet! My name is Jordon Douglas but some call me Jordy or Jojo. I am a former teacher turned front-end developer based in Memphis,TN! Why would I leave working in the school system to develop? Glad you asked. I absolutely love working with children but I was simply not happy. I was unfulfilled and was not being challenged with my skill set I acquired over the years of working with children. So I began searching for alternative career paths while in graduate school. After doing countless google searches, I landed on front-end web development and thought "This is pretty cool". A year later (thanks to one of the kids I mentor), I designed my first website for Lite Memphis! The rest is history!

Outside of programming and figuring out for loops, I absolutely love painting and nature! I also love a good concert or finding a local eatery to make my new spot!
