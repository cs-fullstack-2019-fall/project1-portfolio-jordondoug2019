About Me

My career path started with a passion for counseling at-risk youth. I graduated with a B.S. in Psychology from Tennessee State University and received my Master's of Non-Profit Management from Lipscomb University. Aside from working with youth, I have always had an interest in web design/development and technology. After a chance encounter with a mobile app developer during my graduate school studies, I began my search of understanding web and app development. Fast Forward 5 years and I officially made the jump from In- School Suspension Teacher to a proud graduate of CodeCrew's CodeS School Bootcamp. During my time in the bootcamp, I realized a new passion, using technology to creatively solve our everyday problems. I am still evolving as a developer and looking for new ways to expand my skillset! Grow with me on my journey here !
